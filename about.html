WHO WE ARE

Laura Agbakahi is a passionate and determined person who want to make a difference and who deeply believe that every child matters.

By giving every child the opportunity to receive a quality preschool education, we are giving them a chance to flourish. To know the joy of success.

To believe that they are worthy of their dreams. Early childhood education is the foundation upon which they will stand for the rest of their lives, and the foundation for the future of our world.

Cradle Kinder is focused only for toddler age with the basics of understanding Alhapets, Numbers and Animal Phonics songs. The three major tribes phonic language will also help retain and not erode our mother tongue. We have resolved to work with partners from all over the world to achieve our goal of a quality preschool education for all.

We owe our deepest gratitude to everyone who believes, as we do, in every child

You have our thanks, but more importantly, you have theirs.
